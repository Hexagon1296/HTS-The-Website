Whamageddon

Whamageddon is a challenge that us H-Makers have done for the past 3 years so far. The goal is to reach 25 December without hearing the song "Last Christmas" by Wham, starting from 1 December. If you do get whammed (which is to hear the song), then you loose and are sent to the first level of Whamhalla. Successive whams send you to the next level. Remixes and covers do NOT count against you.

Whams normally occur on the radio, advertisements, or in public places by the staff in said place. Being whammed in any other, unconventional means, like being played in school because someone requested it to (That's real), is called a whambush. Whamming yourself is to commit Whamicide, which is may or may not be a serious Whamageddon crime.

If you are unsure of how many times you have been whammed, then you are in a Whamiposition. Whamipositions, just like classical superpositions (Which is an oxymoron because classical and quantum are opposites), can be weighted towards a certain number of whams.

If you get whammed, well then you have to post #Whamageddon on all platforms to signify that you lost the challenge.
